## Core Thesis

1. **The junior developer job is disappearing.** AI ate the bottom rungs of the ladder.
2. **The top has never been more valuable.** Senior engineering roles pay more than ever precisely because of #1.
3. **Curiosity and gumption separate you, not skill.** Follow-through, serious curiosity, and passion that leaves a trail of evidence.
4. **Use college strategically.** Network, explore broadly, follow through on what interests you. It's about who you know.
